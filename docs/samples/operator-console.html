Action gate (Well Safety Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden by a human approver. Jurisdiction spec-basis, reservoir-pressure window, annular-pressure/MAASP, water cut, H2S/IDLH and integrity-flag ground truth are independently recomputed, never trusted from the advisor's proposal; a real crude lift or production settlement is always a human production superintendent's call, at every rollout phase.

OpGate
:well/intakephase-3 auto-commit when clean, no capital risk yet -- the ONLY auto-eligible op in this domain
:reservoir/assessALWAYS human approval · spec-basis independently checked against crude.facts, never fabricated
:well/liftALWAYS human approval · real-world actuation (opens a real well to flow against a live reservoir) · reservoir-pressure window, annular-pressure/MAASP, water cut, H2S/IDLH, integrity flag and double-lift guard ALL independently re-verified, never auto at any phase
:production/settleALWAYS human approval · real-world actuation (royalty/royalty-volume finalization) · evidence completeness + double-settlement guard independently enforced, never auto at any phase
